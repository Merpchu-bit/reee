Takayan Profile Page

This is a webpage based off a instagram profile of a famous artist that me and a teammate liked. As shown in the video, there is a dropdown menu where you can 'add to close friends', 'unfollow', and 'mute'. If you click on any of the buttons, a popup, will appear and confirm your action. Below the buttons, are images of some music the artist has made. When hovering over the images, they will turn into the Youtube videos, where you can listen to the actual songs. Me and my teammate incorporated HTML, CSS, and Jquery into this project.

Orange Treasure Hunt

This project is called "Orange Hunt!". It's a small digital adventure to get some delicious oranges. The begining goes a follows, and there are two options that you can choose; "Shake the tree!!" and Pick off theoranges. Pick an orange,leads you to the next scene. Here, since your so weak, you need tools to get the oranges. Once you pick the right answer, you go to the next scene. Lastly, you collect your oranges, yet they get napped. The "Shake the tree!!!" option, the story plays out relativly the same, but you'll have some carma for disturbing nature. This project includes HTML, CSS, and Jquery (along with many many hours with it).

Rock Paper Scissors

This project is a silly rock paper scissors game where you go against a computer. As you can see this is no regular rock paper scissors, but a fight to the death! Not really. Anyway, if this epic battle isn't challenging enough then you can press the "want an extra Challenge?" Where an extra character is unlocked for the user to use. Basically it's an instant win for the user.
